Colour Guess!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game

There are four colours. By clicking on a colour, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number, you lose the game if your score goes over the random number

The value of each colour is hidden from you until you click on it

The value of each colour is changed everytime the game starts
